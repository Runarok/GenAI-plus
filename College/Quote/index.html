About Quote Manager

This Quote Manager is a personal exploration project documenting inspirational quotes discovered on doorways and walls throughout our campus building.

The quotes are recorded in the exact order they were found while walking through the main college building from the ground floor to the 4th floor, following a clockwise path via stair entrances.

Each quote includes:

  • The original quote text as it appears
  • The author (when known or attributed)
  • The specific floor(s) where the quote can be found

Use the floor filter to explore quotes from specific levels of the building, or browse them all to follow the complete journey of discovery.
