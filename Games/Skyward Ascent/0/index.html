How to Play

Objective: Climb as high as possible by jumping between handholds!


Controls:

• Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move

• Jump towards orange dots (handholds)

• Don't fall or you'll lose!


Tips:

• Plan your jumps carefully

• The higher you climb, the harder it gets

• Score increases with height!
