What is Meta Prompting?

Meta prompting is a technique where you engage an AI to first refine or optimize your original prompt before using it in another AI conversation or application. This two-stage process lets you:

  • Distill your intent into a clear, powerful prompt tailored to your specific goal, audience, and platform.
  • Achieve more accurate, creative, or context-aware results from the target AI.
  • Iteratively improve prompts, incorporating feedback and new requirements at each step.

How it works:
1. Meta-prompt: Tell the AI to act as a prompt engineer and rewrite your initial prompt for maximum effectiveness.
2. Refined Prompt: Use the AI's improved prompt in the intended context (another AI chat, app, or tool).
3. Iterate: After seeing the output, ask the meta-AI to further refine the prompt based on new needs or feedback.
